About Me

Passionate Biochemist & Researcher

Hello! I'm Mahedi Hassan, a dedicated biochemist from Dhaka, Bangladesh, holding a B.Sc. in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology from Gono Bishwabidyalay.

My academic journey combines experience as a lecturer in medical institutes with a growing research focus on computational drug discovery. I am currently engaged in in-silico research, employing molecular docking, molecular dynamics simulations, and AI-assisted tools to investigate natural compounds as potential therapeutic candidates.

My research interests lie at the interface of molecular biology, chemistry, and computational modeling, where predictive simulations can accelerate and refine drug development strategies.

I strongly believe that the future of biomedical research lies in the integration of wet-lab experimentation and computational innovation. Beyond academic work, I am an avid reader and advocate for science education and public health awareness.
